Our Vision

ORBS concept is to deliver incredible lifestyle experiences using new, “smart” software technology that can think and take action. Each of us will have an ORB as our go-to assistant for information, resources, human engagement, collaboration and personal expression.

About Us

ORBS Inc is a new software technology venture. We are creating ORBS, a world of 3D intelligent avatars that solve our daily problems and eliminate the need for websites, blogs, and other conventional web services.

What are ORBS?

An ORB is a new form of AI software called an "autonomous intelligent agent.” An ORB is a practical, readily-available vehicle for quickly and effectively addressing speciﬁc user objectives. Users will have the ability to identify and select a basic ORB from the ORBS App, customize it with performance features that can accomplish their objective and launch it into action.

The World of ORBS

ORBS will be available for a wide range of applications. All will possess common features including "smart functionality", an "intelligent" user interface, pro-active user-driven engagement, enhanced interaction, and innumerable enhancement options.

ORBS will be ubiquitous because they will be simple and free to create and use. They will be omnipresent, always at our command and call. They will be a form of digital consciousness capable of "thinking" on our behalf and taking effective action as well as engaging with other conscious devices such as other ORBS, software, entities and intelligent components of automobiles and computers.

ORBS is an emerging world of cloud-based digital intelligent agents that can help us express ourselves in new ways to accomplish our objectives.

ORBS are powerful, proactive and highly mobile tools we will use to facilitate personal interaction and engagement, our involvement in business and commerce and our pursuit of lifestyle activities. They will eliminate the need for traditional websites, blogs and Google searches, which are static and reactive rather than dynamic and proactive. These and other traditional web-centric devices will no longer be of value as the era of "intelligent agents" empowers us and transforms our lives.

ORBS will sharpen our focus and the power of our intention to bring us new, more beneﬁcial lifestyle solutions and alternatives. They will act to bring our objectives and interests to reality, amplifying and implementing our actions on a broader scale, connecting us with others in important new ways, and anticipating possibilities we may not have known about or considered.
